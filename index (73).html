Earthquake Tracker FAQ

Where can I see earthquakes today?

EpicenterLive shows earthquakes today on a live, interactive global earthquake map and an automatically updating earthquake feed, sourced from USGS, EMSC, GeoNet, INGV, KOERI, BGS, and GA. The map and list refresh continuously so you can follow recent earthquakes as they happen.

How do I find an earthquake near me?

Use the Search Location filter above the earthquake feed to type a city, region, or country. The earthquake tracker will filter recent earthquake activity to that area so you can quickly check for an earthquake near you.

How often is the earthquake data updated?

The earthquake feed, live earthquake map, and statistics refresh automatically about every 30 seconds, pulling the latest earthquakes and earthquake updates from multiple official seismological networks.

What do the earthquake magnitude colors mean on the map?

On the global earthquake map, marker colors represent magnitude ranges from minor (under M3) to major (M7+). Larger, brighter markers indicate stronger earthquakes, making it easy to spot significant earthquake activity today at a glance.

Can I get earthquake alerts and notifications?

Yes. Tap the notification bell in the live ticker to enable browser-based earthquake alerts. You will receive instant updates when a significant earthquake is detected anywhere in the world.

Which data sources power this earthquake monitor?

EpicenterLive aggregates earthquake information from multiple official sources, including the USGS (United States Geological Survey), EMSC, GeoNet, INGV, KOERI (Kandilli Observatory, Turkey), BGS (British Geological Survey), and GA (Geoscience Australia), to provide a comprehensive world earthquake tracker.

Deprem son dakika bilgilerini nereden takip edebilirim?

EpicenterLive üzerinden anlık deprem haritasını ve son dakika deprem listesini takip edebilirsiniz. Veriler USGS, EMSC, GeoNet, INGV, KOERI (Kandilli Rasathanesi), BGS ve GA gibi kaynaklardan otomatik olarak yaklaşık 30 saniyede bir güncellenir.
